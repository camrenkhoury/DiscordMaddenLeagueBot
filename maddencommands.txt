MADDEN DISCORD BOT — COMMAND LIST
================================

!addplayer <name>
Adds a new player to the league with a 0–0 record and no points.

!removeplayer <name>
(Admin only) Removes a player ONLY if they have 0 games played and no recorded stats.

!game <player1> <score1> <player2> <score2>
Records a regular-season game between two existing players and updates standings.
If playoff mode is active, this instead attempts to record a valid playoff game.

!standings
Displays the current regular-season standings, sorted by wins, losses, and point differential.

!resetseason
(Admin only) Resets the current season standings and games.
Players remain, but all records are cleared and the season number increments.
Cannot be used during playoff mode.

!playoffmode
(Admin only) Locks regular-season standings and starts playoff mode.
Generates and locks the playoff bracket.

!currentplayoff
Displays the current playoff bracket, including:
- Play-In (if applicable)
- Round 1
- Round 2 (bye teams)
- Super Bowl path
Shown as a visual ASCII bracket.

--------------------------------
NOTES
--------------------------------
- Play-In games only exist if the total number of teams is odd.
- Seeds 1 and 2 receive first-round byes and appear in Round 2.
- Playoff games must match the bracket or they will be rejected.
- Regular-season standings are locked once playoff mode begins.
